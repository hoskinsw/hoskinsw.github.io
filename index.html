How to use these templates

  1. Click on one of the templates above to see how it looks and functions.
  2. Once you decide on the layout you like, navigate to that specific folder (template1, template2, or template3) in your project repository.
  3. Open the index.html file for that template.
  4. Replace the bracketed placeholder text (e.g., [Your Name]) with your actual information.
  5. Upload your resume and project screenshots into the files folder to link them correctly.
